TAROT, LOL

A goofy, experimental way to read tarot online.

The goal of this site is to maximize the associative meanings you might discover for the cards you draw.

If you don't know anything about tarot, that's fine! Hit the button, click on some cards, see what happens. Who knows what you might discover.


This tool is built and maintained by Charlie Trochlil using D3 skills learned from Ben Artuso in a semester course at Forge.
