TAROT, LOL

A goofy, experimental way to read tarot.

The goal of this site is to give beginner and mid-level tarot readers reminders not just of the cards' meanings, but how they associate with other cards in the deck.

If you don't know anything about tarot, that's fine! Hit the button, click on some cards, see if you get a sense of it.


Click on any of your drawn cards to get information about that card.
